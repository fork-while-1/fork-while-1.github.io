fork-while-1@github.io:~$ Why this blog
Being in tech for around 7 years now, I've come to realize how pretty much everything can be optimized. And after a long struggle with various issues, and watching other people struggle with their own issues, a couple years ago I finally came to the realization that the same goes with your life. Since then, one of my main hobbies has been optimizing various aspects of my life—mood, happiness, physical health, and just overall well-being. Life is too short to not live it to your fullest, hence my decision to milk the most from it!
Of course, almost everyone will tell you to start with the basics—eat healthy, exercise, sleep well, etc. However, there is much more to the art of optimization than this, and there are a lot of quick fixes that don't take as much effort as the steps listed above, but will, over-time, make a noticeable difference in the quality of your life. And by noticeable, I really mean noticeable—my personal life has had a night and day difference from what it used to be.
The rest of this blog will be organized in small articles that cover different aspects of life, and will include steps I or people I know follow to optimize those aspects. Note that this is not meant to be a comprehensive blog, it's just something I work on in my free time. For more comprehensive resources with citations to relevant academic papers, please follow Leo and Longevity on YouTube, which is perhaps the single most academically rigorous channel I've seen on bio-hacking and longevity. Feel free to skip to sections you're most interested in. And as always, don't hesitate to reach out with any questions!
